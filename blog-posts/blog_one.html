    Creativity is the act of turning new and imaginative ideas into reality. Creativity is characterised by the ability to perceive the world in new ways, to find hidden patterns, to make connections between seemingly unrelated phenomena, and to generate solutions. Creativity involves two processes: thinking, then producing.

    Begin with the end in mind

    Focus time and energy on things and situations that can be controlled by you. So, what do you want to be when you are an old woman or man? How do you want to be perceived by others?

    • Think about it for a moment.
    • Write it down.
    • Visualize it.

    Are you–right now–who you want to be, what you dreamed you’d be, doing what you always wanted to do? Be honest.
    To reach your mission you have to be proactiv. Proactive means tending to iniiate change rather than reacting to events, feelings or actions of other people. Take control and make things happen rather than just adjust to a situation or wait for something to happen.
